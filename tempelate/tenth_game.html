Ichigo Kurosaki


The Reluctant Hero of Bleach


Ichigo Kurosaki is the iconic protagonist of Tite Kubo’s renowned manga and anime series Bleach. A seemingly ordinary high school student, Ichigo’s life changes forever when he encounters a Soul Reaper named Rukia Kuchiki. Through this fateful meeting, he inherits her powers and becomes a substitute Soul Reaper, tasked with protecting both the living and spirit worlds from dark forces.
